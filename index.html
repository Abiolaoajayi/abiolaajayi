Parental Influence on Undernutrition in Egypt (2014)

This project investigates the effect of parental education and occupation on undernutrition among children under five in Egypt, based on the 2014 Demographic and Health Survey (DHS). Using anthropometric indicators such as height-for-age (stunting), weight-for-age (underweight), and weight-for-height (wasting), derived from children's weight, height, and BMI-related Z-scores, the study identifies malnourished children. A rural-urban comparison was performed to highlight geographic disparities and support policy recommendations.
