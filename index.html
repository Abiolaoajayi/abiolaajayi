Walmart Stock Forecasting (ARIMA, SARIMA, ARCH, GARCH)

Built multiple time-series models (ARIMA, SARIMA, ARCH, GARCH) to forecast Walmart’s adjusted close stock prices using 2025 daily trading data. Models evaluated short-term trends, seasonality, and volatility clustering for actionable insights.

  • ARIMA(10,1,0): Stabilization around ~$85; uncertainty widens with horizon.
  • SARIMA(1,1,1)(1,1,1,5): Seasonality captured; modest decline expected.
  • ARCH(1): Short-term shocks; limited persistence fit.
  • GARCH(1,1): Strong persistence; volatility up ~2.28% → 2.42%.
